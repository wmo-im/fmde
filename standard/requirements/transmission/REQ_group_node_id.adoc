[[req_transmission-group_node_id]]
[width="90%",cols="2,6a"]
|===
^|*Requirement {counter:req-id}* |*/req/transmission/group_node_id*
^|A |A group-id or node-id SHALL consist only of lowercase ASCII alphanumeric characters and hyphens (-).
^|B |A group-id or node-id SHALL NOT begin or end with a hyphen.
^|C |A group-id or node-id SHALL NOT be empty.
^|D |A node-id SHALL be unique within the namespace of its associated group-id.
|===
