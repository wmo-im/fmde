[[req_transmission-protocol]]
[width="90%",cols="2,6a"]
|===
^|*Requirement {counter:req-id}* |*/req/transmission/protocol*
^|A |MQTT SHALL be used for all Publish-Subscribe workflows.
^|B |The version of MQTT used SHALL be https://docs.oasis-open.org/mqtt/mqtt/v5.0/mqtt-v5.0.html[v5.0.0].
^|C |Message Encodings SHALL be published using a QoS level of **2** to ensure that they are delivered and received exactly once.
^|D |Client connections to a given MQTT broker SHALL set Receive Maximum to **1** when establishing the session.
|===
